# Risk Register

## Purpose

Track delivery risks that can affect milestone scope, quality, or schedule.

## Status Scale

- `Open`: active risk without full mitigation
- `Monitoring`: mitigation exists, keep tracking
- `Closed`: no longer relevant

## Impact Scale

- `High`: can block milestone or release
- `Medium`: causes rework or quality drop
- `Low`: limited scope impact

## Probability Scale

- `High`: likely in current sprint
- `Medium`: possible, needs watch
- `Low`: unlikely but known

## Update Cadence

- Update at sprint planning
- Re-check at sprint retrospective
- Add entries when new blockers appear
